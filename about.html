Two decades of coconut shell craft, now exported worldwide.

Java Briquettes Co. was founded on a simple belief: Indonesia's coconut shells, properly carbonized and controlled, produce the cleanest-burning charcoal in the world.

Our Story

From a family kiln in East Java to a 120-container export operation.

Founded in 2011 in Sidoarjo, East Java, Java Briquettes Co. began as a small carbonization workshop supplying local domestic markets. As demand for cleaner-burning, sulfur-free charcoal grew abroad, we invested in mechanized briquette pressing, an in-house QC laboratory, and export documentation expertise.

Today we operate two production facilities, work with over 40 verified coconut-shell collection partners across Java and Sulawesi, and ship more than 120 containers annually to shisha, BBQ and wholesale brands in over 30 countries.
