Home / Export Process

From inquiry to your warehouse — fully transparent.

Six stages, clear timelines, and a dedicated export coordinator for every order.

  1. 1Day 1–2

    Inquiry & Quotation

    Submit your product, volume and destination port. Receive a formal proforma invoice within 24 hours.

  2. 2Day 3–7

    Sample Approval

    Free samples shipped via courier for lab and burn-time evaluation on your end.

  3. 3Week 2–4

    Production

    Order enters the production queue with scheduled QC checkpoints at each stage.

  4. 4Week 4

    Pre-Shipment QC

    Final lab testing and optional third-party inspection (SGS / Bureau Veritas) before loading.

  5. 5Week 4–5

    Loading & Shipping

    Container loading, customs declaration, and Bill of Lading issued within 48 hours of departure.

  6. 6Week 5–9

    Delivery

    Tracking updates provided through transit to arrival at your destination port.

Shipping Terms

Flexible Incoterms for every buyer.

FOB

Free on Board — Surabaya (Tanjung Perak) or Jakarta (Tanjung Priok) ports.

CNF

Cost & Freight to your destination port, freight pre-arranged by us.

CIF

Cost, Insurance & Freight — full coverage from our port to yours.

EXW

Ex Works from our Sidoarjo facility for buyers managing their own freight.
