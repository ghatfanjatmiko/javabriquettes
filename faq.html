Ordering & Pricing

Our standard MOQ is one 20ft container (approx. 18–20 metric tons). Mixed-product containers can be arranged for qualified buyers ordering multiple SKUs.

Pricing is quoted per metric ton, based on Incoterm (FOB/CNF/CIF), product format and packaging. Volume discounts apply from 3+ containers per order.

Shipping & Logistics

We regularly ship FOB, CNF and CIF from Surabaya (Tanjung Perak) and Jakarta (Tanjung Priok) ports, with EXW available on request.

Production typically takes 2–3 weeks after sample approval, with ocean transit adding 3–6 weeks depending on your destination port.

Quality & Certification

Our facility operates under ISO 9001, HACCP and FSC-aligned sourcing standards, with SGS pre-shipment inspection available on request.
