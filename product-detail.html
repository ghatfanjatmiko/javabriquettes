Why This Product

Precision-cut for consistent lounge experience.

Uneven cube sizing is the most common complaint from shisha operators — it leads to inconsistent heat and coal replacement mid-session. Our extrusion and cutting line holds a tolerance of ±0.5mm, so every cube performs identically.

  • Sulfur-free, no chemical odor
  • Minimal crackling and spark
  • Consistent white-ash coating within 8–10 minutes
